FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS,
ANSWERED HONESTLY.

What exactly does HOI do — and how is it different from a typical agency?+
HOI builds complete business systems — combining strategy, technology, and marketing execution under one accountable team. Unlike traditional agencies that deliver one service and step back, HOI owns your outcomes across the full growth stack: AI automation, business digitisation, startup builds, digital marketing, and revenue leverage. One team, one point of contact, one set of results to measure.
How long does a typical project take from first call to launch?+
Startup builds typically go live within 90 days. Business digitisation and automation projects show measurable results within 60 days. Digital marketing campaigns produce meaningful ROI within 60–90 days. Every engagement begins with a free 45-minute strategy call and a written 3-day growth audit before any scoping begins.
Is there a lock-in contract?+
No. HOI uses fixed-scope, fixed-price project contracts with milestone-based payments. After project completion, any ongoing engagement is month-to-month with 30 days notice to exit. 94% of HOI clients continue beyond their first project — because the results are measurable, not because they're contractually obligated to stay.
Do you work with small businesses, or only large enterprises?+
HOI's primary market is Australian SMEs and startups — which represent 99% of all Australian businesses (Australian Bureau of Statistics). We work with businesses from pre-revenue startups through to $20M+ revenue companies. The unifying factor is ambition: clients who want measurable outcomes, not just deliverables.
What industries does HOI work with?+
HOI has delivered projects across 14 industries including retail, health technology, professional services, construction and trades, finance, logistics, education, property, and government. Our methodology is industry-agnostic — what varies is the specific execution strategy per industry.
What is the typical cost of working with HOI?+
All projects are fixed-price with a written scope before any commitment. We provide a free written growth audit before scoping — so you always know exactly what you're buying and why before signing anything. Book a free strategy call to discuss your specific situation and get an honest estimate for your project.
